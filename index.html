Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23. 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application (“GrizAI”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication (including Google Sign-In) to identify users. This includes name, email, and profile photo.
  • Subscription Information: Payment is handled through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store credit card details.
  • Camera & Photos: Access is used to analyze food images and return nutritional information. Images are not stored.
  • Usage Data: We collect basic usage info to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Provide and improve app features
  • Process subscriptions
  • Return nutrition analysis

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with trusted third-party services such as Firebase for authentication, and Google Play or Apple App Store for subscription management. Please refer to Firebase's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for more details. We use OpenAI’s API to analyze food images and generate nutritional information. If you enable our AI-powered features, inputs and outputs (such as text and images) may be shared with OpenAI to provide and improve their services. OpenAI may temporarily store this data for safety and research purposes. You can learn more by reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. Security

We do not store images processed by the app. They are only used temporarily for nutritional analysis during your session and are deleted once the analysis is complete. Images are sent to OpenAI for analysis during your session. They are not stored on our servers. OpenAI may retain this data temporarily to improve its models, as explained in their Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion or updates of your data by emailing us at dbozicbusiness@gmail.com.

6. Children

This app is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app or on this page.

8. Contact

Email: dbozicbusiness@gmail.com
